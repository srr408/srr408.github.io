A Sketch of A Pandemic

Introduction

The following infographics showcases the impact of the COVID-19 Pandemic in two-fold ways. First, a global overview that illustrates the total number of cases, total Covid-19 deaths, total number of cases and total number of deaths scaled to country. Please note, the term country* here is used in a very broad and inclusive manner. Some of the portrayed regions' status as a sovereign country is disputed, and therefore, will not appear in lists that are more stringent with the definiton of a country. Second, the infographics will focus on the United States of America. The country's combination of a highly politicized COVID response, advanced scientific basis, and developed economy makes it an interesting data set. Having situated USA in a global locus in the first part, the second part permits the audience to appreciate the time progress of this outbreak at more granular state-by-state detail. Enjoy!
